Weapons

Melee => Higher combo more damage, especialy on heavy attack

Ranged => Slain Enemies and containers drop ammo

Energy => slain Enemies drop energy

    Recommended mods (reduce energy cost) :
  • Streamline (mod)
  • Fleeting Expertise (mod)

Movement

Bullet jump => (crouch + jump) or (slide + jump) => Fast forward jump

Aim glide => (while in the air + aim) => slower time and glide in the air
