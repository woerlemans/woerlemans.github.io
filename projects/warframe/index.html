End Game Goals

Railjack: Create your own spaceship, recruit a squad, and set off across an entirely new Star Chart.

Endless Missions: Test your builds by playing endless Survival, Defense, Disruption, and much more.

Steel Path: Complete the Star Chart once again in Warframe's version of New Game Plus, complete with unique rewards.

Syndicates: Increase your reputation with various open world factions to unlock exclusive weapons and cosmetics.

Fashion Frame: Create the best-looking Warframe possible with Warframe's extensive customization options.

Trading: Exchange valuable Prime items, mods, and more for Platinum, the game's premium currency.
